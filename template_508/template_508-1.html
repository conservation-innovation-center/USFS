Tree cover includes all trees occurring on all land uses, such as individual trees found over turf, impervious, agricultural, wetlands, or other lands. It also includes areas of "forest,” defined in this dataset as patches of tree cover 1 acre or greater, with a minimum patch width of 240 feet.
2.
Other includes a mixture of non-treed land uses not captured in the main pie chart categories. See the Data Guide for detailed definitions of “other” and all the land use categories.
